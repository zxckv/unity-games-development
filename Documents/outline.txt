Genre:
 - First-person shooter
 - Wave survivor

Inspiration: 
 - Call of Duty Zombies

Goals:
 - Set new high round records
 - Unlock, explore and 'finish' maps
 - Complete challenges

Progression (In level):
 - Buy player upgrades
 - Unlock new weapons 
 - Upgrade weapons
 - Finish maps
 - Find collectables

Progression (Out of level):
 - Gain experience and level up (unlocks new items)
 - Unlock new maps
 - Unlock customisation
